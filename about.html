Hi, I'm Niraj! 🌿

Professional Mehndi Artist — Mant

Welcome to Niraj With Mehndi! With over 3 years of experience in the art of mehndi, I have had the privilege of creating beautiful designs for 150+ brides and countless other clients.

My journey started with a passion for this traditional art form, and today I specialize in Bridal, Arabic, and modern mehndi styles. Every design I create is unique — tailored to your personal style and the special occasion.

I use only 100% natural and organic mehndi, ensuring a rich, dark stain that lasts 7-10 days with proper care. Hygiene and professionalism are my top priorities.
