📄 Booking & Cancellation Policy

Please read before booking

📋 Booking Process

  1. Browse our gallery and select your favorite designs
  2. Fill the booking form with your details
  3. Receive confirmation on WhatsApp
  4. Pay 20% advance to confirm your slot
  5. Get the beautiful mehndi on your event day!

💰 Payment Terms

  • 20% advance at the time of booking confirmation
  • Remaining 80% on the day of the event
  • Payment accepted via UPI and cash
  • Payment details will be shared on WhatsApp

❌ Cancellation Policy

  • 48+ hours before event: Full advance refund
  • 24-48 hours before event: 50% advance refund
  • Less than 24 hours: No refund of advance
  • Rescheduling is free (subject to availability)

🚗 Travel Policy

  • Service within Mant city limits is free
  • Outside city: Travel charges apply based on distance
  • Travel charges will be communicated before booking confirmation
  • Artist arrives 30 minutes before scheduled time

⏰ Timing & Delays

  • Please be ready at the scheduled time to avoid delays
  • Delays from client side may affect the design completion
  • Artist will wait maximum 30 minutes beyond scheduled time
